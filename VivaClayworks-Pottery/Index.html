About Viva Clayworks

At Viva Clayworks, we’re a family of passionate “cerama-siblings” who believe in the joy of creating with clay. Our Edmonton studio is home to twelve pottery wheels, plenty of creative space, and an open, welcoming community where everyone can get their hands muddy and discover the art of pottery.

Founded in 1995 as a small branch of Clayworks Studio, our space just off Whyte Avenue has grown into a local hub for clay enthusiasts. Here, you’ll find artists exploring hand-building, wheel throwing, and everything in between.
