What is Firing?

Firing is the process of heating clay in a kiln to turn it from soft, fragile material into durable ceramic. Most pieces are fired twice — first for bisque firing, which hardens the clay, and then for glaze firing, which melts the glaze into a smooth, glassy finish. It’s what transforms your handmade clay into lasting pottery.

Kiln Firing Services:
Have your pieces ready but need access to a kiln? We can help! Bring your kiln-ready work to us and we’ll fire it for you.

  • Pricing: Firing costs are based on the space your pieces take up in the kiln for cone 04, 06, and 6 firings. We’ll measure your pieces and provide an estimate. For other firing needs, charges are based on the kiln load.
  • Clay Type: Please know the type of clay you are bringing. Unsure? Bring a photo of the packaging.
  • Labeling: Clearly and legibly sign each piece before dropping it off.

For questions or to schedule a firing, call or email us today!
