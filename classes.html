Pottery Night Experience: 90 Minute Wheel Event

$75 class fee

Join us Saturdays and Sundays at 4 pm or 6 pm for an hour and a half wheel-throwing class designed for adults. You’ll follow a step-by-step demo with our instructor to create your own pottery piece, then choose your glaze color. You make the design, and we’ll handle the firing and finishing!

Your event fee includes one finished piece (extra pieces $15 each). If you make a mug, we can add a handle for $5.
